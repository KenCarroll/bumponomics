# 1.2.7 Ways-of-Organizing

*Organisms (including humans) deploy specific ways-of-organizing depending upon their place (context), their state-of-being, and their problematic situations. These organizational modes include coordinating, cooperating, collaborating, and competing. All are valid, and most real-world situations require a hybrid mix of these.*
