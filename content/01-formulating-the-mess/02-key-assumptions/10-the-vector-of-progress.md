# 1.2.10 The Vector of Progress

*Problems drive action. Our choice of problems and how we tackle them determine our progress. Because Progress is a vector, it can result in either an improvement to our state-of-being (positive trajectory) or an impairment to our state-of-being (negative trajectory).*

*(This chapter is currently being drafted to explore the deep philosophical mechanics of this assumption)*
