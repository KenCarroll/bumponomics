# 1.2.1 The Micro Level: The Individual & The Firm

At the atomic layer of our current system, the rules are dictated by neoclassical economics, which models human beings as *Homo economicus*—perfectly rational agents who always act to maximize their own personal utility.

## The Disconnect of Agency

Because the reigning paradigm measures success purely in terms of financial artifacts (wages, profits, shareholder returns), the Micro level enforces a strict separation between the people who *experience* problems and the people who possess the *agency* to solve them. 

Firms are legally and structurally bound to optimize for profit maximization. When an employee or a consumer experiences friction, the firm does not view this friction as an energetic resource to be transformed into progress. Instead, it views it either as an 'externality' to be ignored, or merely a variable to be extracted for profit and patched with a 'solution.'

By optimizing the Micro level solely for financial artifacts, we paralyze the natural thermodynamic engine of progress. Individuals are stripped of the capacity to alter their environment, and organizations become highly efficient machines for freezing progress into capital.
