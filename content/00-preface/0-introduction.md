Before we dive into the deep mechanics of BUMPS, HUMPS, Cybernetics, and the mathematical vectors of progress, we must first get our foundations right. You cannot build a shared reality without first establishing the shared rules of engagement.

The initial chapters of this Preface exist to explicitly define our foundational meta-frame:

1. **The Catalyst**: Why this journey was undertaken and the necessity of taking the "Red Pill."
2. **Our Observations**: How we currently perceive reality as a shared construct.
3. **The Assumptions**: The constraints and rules that bind our universe.
4. **The Problem Space**: Why the trap of "Solutionism" is mathematically failing society.
5. **The Target Outcomes**: What success looks like when we adopt Problem-Transforming Economics.

Let us establish the foundations.
