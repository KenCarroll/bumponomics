# Gross Meaningful Problems (GMP)

The Measure of Ambition.

Traditional economics counts every dollar spent as equal. Bumponomics weights activity by "Meaning."

Calculation: The sum of resources allocated to HUMPS (Strategic/Mission-critical problems) vs. LUMPS (Maintenance).

Goal: Shift society from Low-GMP (fixing trivialities) to High-GMP (transforming existential threats).

## Ratio of HUMPS to BUMPS

Used to measure the breakdown between overarching HUMPS and the BUMPS that ultimately transform them
