# Evolving (E)

When you are evolving, you are growing through the problem. You are learning from the problem. You are maturing from the problem. You are transforming from the problem. You are maturing into a new state of being. The problem transforms us. The outcomes from the problem transformed because ESSENTIAL for the very existance (Survival) of the system transforming it. Think of it like this: If the problem transforms the system, then the system transforms the problem.  They co-exist and transform each other. The problem remains..but now the problem is crucial for the very existance (Survival) of the system transforming upon which it now depends. How is this different to keeping the problem in place i.e. revolving? Well in Revolving the problem is not transformed. It is externally maintained to the detriment of others. 
When you transform the problem you no longer need to actively maintain it. It's just part of you. An enabler of your growth & development.

## Diagnostic Question

**“Is the problem part of a broader transition?”**

---

## Definition

Evolving treats the problem as **fuel for learning and system growth**, allowing new capabilities to emerge over time.

> The problem transforms us.

---

## Essence

**Grow through it.**

---

## Key Characteristics

* 🌱 Developmental
* ⏳ Time‑based
* 🔁 Feedback‑driven
* 🧭 Directional change

---

## Typical Signals

* “We’re becoming better because of this”
* “The system is maturing”
* “This is shaping our next phase”

---

## Examples

* Startups learning from early failures
* Societal norms shifting
* Skill growth through difficulty

---

## Counter‑Plays

* **Against Revolving:** Separate learning from exploitation.
* **Risk:** Endless evolution without closure.

---

## System Effect

The problem fades as the system outgrows it.
