# Bumponomics: The Economics of Problem Transformation

## 1. Abstract (< 250 words)

**Title: Beyond GDP: Re-imagining Economics as Problem Transformation**

Our current economic operating system is critically flawed. By relying on **GDP** (Gross Domestic Product), we measure the _volume_ of transactions but ignore their _value_ to human viability. A divorce creates legal fees; a natural disaster compels reconstruction spending—both drive GDP up, yet both represent system failures. We are effectively flying the spaceship Earth with a speedometer but no navigation system.

This paper introduces **Bumponomics**, a new economic paradigm that re-purposes human systems towards **Problem Transformation**. We posit that the fundamental unit of value is not the dollar, but the **Transformed Problem**.

Using the **PTO Process** (Problems ➔ Transformations ➔ Outcomes), Bumponomics provides the "physics" for this new economy. It replaces the blind pursuit of growth with three evolutionary metrics:

1.  **GMP (Gross Meaningful Problems):** Measuring our ambition—are we allocating resources to existential threats or trivialities?
2.  **GPO (Gross Positive Outcomes):** Measuring our efficacy—did our solutions actually solve the problem without creating negative displacement?
3.  **GIVE (Gross Increased Variety of Environment):** Measuring our legacy—did we expand the "library of parts" and future evolutionary potential?

By shifting our focus from _spending_ (GDP) to _solving_ (GMP/GPO/GIVE), we can transition from an Economy of More to an **Economy of Better**.

## 2. Poster Design & Outline

**Theme:** "The Upgrade." Visuals should contrast the "Old OS" (Linear, Blind, Transaction-heavy) with the "New OS" (Circular, Directed, Evolutionary).

### Section 1: The Glitch (The Old Economy)

- **Headline:** THE GDP FALLACY
- **Concept:** "We are measuring the noise, not the signal."
- **Visual:** A "Broken Compass" or a graph showing GDP rising while "Wellbeing/Viability" lines flatline or decline.
- **Narrative:** GDP counts cancer treatments and car accidents as "growth." It has a **Problem Blindspot**—it cannot distinguish between bad problems (disasters) and good problems (investments).

### Section 2: The Patch (The New Purpose)

- **Headline:** ALL SYSTEMS ARE PROBLEM TRANSFORMING
- **Core Thesis:** The true purpose of any economy is not to generate capital, but to **Transform Problems into Progress**.
- **The Engine:** **The PTO Process**
  - **Problems (Input):** The raw material of progress.
  - **Transformations (Action):** The application of energy/strategy (The 6 Strategies: _Absolve, Resolve, Dissolve, Solve, Evolve, Exploit_).
  - **Outcomes (Output):** The new reality (OOSAWID).

### Section 3: The Dashboard (New Metrics)

- **Headline:** MEASURING WHAT MATTERS
- Replacing GDP with the **Triple-G** Dashboard:
  1.  **GMP (Input):** **Gross Meaningful Problems.**
      - _Are we working on things that matter?_ Tracks the shift from trivial "LUMPS" to existential "HUMPS."
  2.  **GPO (Throughout):** **Gross Positive Outcomes.**
      - _Did it work?_ Measures net positive vector (Transformation minus Negative Displacement).
  3.  **GIVE (Legacy):** **Gross Increased Variety of Environment.**
      - _Did we evolve?_ Measures the increase in options, tools, and resilience (The "Library of Parts").

### Section 4: The Result (Human Impact)

- **Headline:** FROM SURVIVING TO THRIVING
- **Metric:** **PDST (State of Being)**
  - Moving populations from **D**emise ➔ **S**urvival ➔ **T**hriving.
- **Visual:** A "Heatmap" of society showing the shift in State of Being as the PTO process resolves existential BUMPS.
- **Call to Action:** "Upgrade the OS. Join the **Problem Transforming Economy**."
