# Establishing Our Common Ground



-In the introduction have unique perspectives,Here is my personal perspectives: 
- Earth is akin to our common lifeboat. We all have to share it and look after it. And we don’t have any other place to go.
- It ability to support life as we know it is being threatened. If we had a global dashboard for well-being its warning lights would be flashing.
- As a parent the future well-being of our children looks to be at risk.
- Our behaviours can be controlled through our senses. What we think and do can be ‘nudged’ by others if they know how. These ‘tricks’ are regularly used in to influence our behaviours.
- We like to form into groups. And regularly participate in many groups at the same time e.g. family, community, nationality, business. Group dynamics can influence individual behaviour. 
- Individuals can follow the crowd to focus on whatever the group shines a light on. 
- Many individuals and groups behaviour are being dominated by an abstract concept call ‘the economy’. 
- Economics has become our de facto Social Operating System. Its language, beliefs, values, processes, and goals has invaded every aspect of society. 
- Economics is not improving the world around me. Long standing problems are not being addressed. New hazardous problems are being created. It seems to be taking us in a harmful direction. 
- Economic thinking looks increasingly outdated. It does not seem to be able to respond to the changing conditions.
