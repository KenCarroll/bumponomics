# PROMPT LAB

Welcome to the **BUMPS Prompt Lab**. 

This document is designed as a collaborative scratchpad. Instead of firing off raw commands to an AI image generator and hoping for the best, use this space to deliberately design, combine, and refine your visual metaphors. 

By keeping this file in the root directory (outside of the `content/` folder), it will never be compiled by VitePress or appear in the live book.

---

## 🛑 The Golden Rule: Paint What Is Seen, Not What It Means

AI image generators do not understand philosophy, sociology, or metaphors. When we prompt them with terms like *"systems in motion,"* *"human agency organizing,"* or *"formations of energy,"* the AI gets deeply confused and tries to literalize those abstract concepts (e.g., drawing literal flames for "energy," or weird chimneys for "focus"). 

To get perfect results, **you must strip all metaphorical meaning out of the prompt.** Describe strictly what shadows, shapes, textures, and physical objects you want drawn.

### ❌ Bad Prompt (Abstract/Metaphorical)
*"Where the plates (systems) and dots (people) collide, the ground buckles upward into a Problematic Formation (BUMP) where people focus their energies."*

### ✅ Good Prompt (Strictly Visual/Physical)
*"Two thick, concrete isometric slabs are colliding. At the collision line, the concrete forcefully buckles upward, forming a smooth, conical mound. Clustered exclusively around the base of this mound are hundreds of tiny, distinct geometric shapes (triangles, squares, circles)."*

**Draft 1 (Original Concept):**
- A simple, minimalist, black and white hand-drawn architectural sketch. 
- We see a wide isometric view of a landscape. 
- The landscape is comprised for different zones that form moving plates. These 'plates' are metaphors for different 'systems' that are in motion relative to each other.
- Upon the plates there are cohorts of dots, representing 'people'. They could be represented by different shapes such as square, circles, triangles, and different shades of grey to represent different groups of people. 
- Where the plates (systems) and dots (people) collide formations (BUMPS) emerge
- There will be multiple BUMPS in the landscape, each representing a different type of problematic situation 
- Where they collide, the ground buckles upward into a massive molehills 'Problematic Formation' (a BUMP). - This formation represents where people focus their energies.
- Swarms of dots (human agency) are attempting to organize on around these new formations of energy. The cohorts may be in conflict with each other or working together to tackle the different BUMPS. 
- The substrate of the landscape is comprised of gears, grids, and other industrial elements representing the 'stuff' of the world.
