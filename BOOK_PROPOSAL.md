---

## 1. OVERVIEW
**The Entrepreneur is dead. Long live the Bumpivore.**

We have been lied to. Traditional business education teaches us that problems are errors to be corrected. We are taught to build "Fixed Organizations"—stable structures designed to solve a problem, productize the solution, and then maximize efficiency. This model assumes that once a problem is solved, the world stabilizes.

**BUMPONOMICS** exposes this as a dangerous illusion.

Because the cycle of **BUMPS** is infinite, the "Fixed Organization" is obsolete. It is rigid in a turbulent world. To survive, we must build **Problem Transforming Organisations (PTOs)**.

**BUMPONOMICS** is the manifesto and the manual for a new economic era. It introduces:
* **The Framework:** A rigorous taxonomy of friction (LUMPS, BUMPS, HUMPS).
* **The Engine:** The Co-Intelligence Matrix (HI + AI).
* **The Metric:** Replacing GDP with **GIVE** (Gross Increased Variety of Environment).
