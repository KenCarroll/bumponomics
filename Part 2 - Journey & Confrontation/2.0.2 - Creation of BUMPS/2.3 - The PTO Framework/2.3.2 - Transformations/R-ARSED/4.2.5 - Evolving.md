# Evolving (E)

## Diagnostic Question

**“Is the problem part of a broader transition?”**

---

## Definition

Evolving treats the problem as **fuel for learning and system growth**, allowing new capabilities to emerge over time.

> The problem transforms us.

---

## Essence

**Grow through it.**

---

## Key Characteristics

* 🌱 Developmental
* ⏳ Time‑based
* 🔁 Feedback‑driven
* 🧭 Directional change

---

## Typical Signals

* “We’re becoming better because of this”
* “The system is maturing”
* “This is shaping our next phase”

---

## Examples

* Startups learning from early failures
* Societal norms shifting
* Skill growth through difficulty

---

## Counter‑Plays

* **Against Revolving:** Separate learning from exploitation.
* **Risk:** Endless evolution without closure.

---

## System Effect

The problem fades as the system outgrows it.
