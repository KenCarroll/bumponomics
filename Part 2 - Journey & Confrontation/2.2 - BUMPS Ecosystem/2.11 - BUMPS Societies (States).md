# BUMPS Socities (States)

The role of the BUMPS "States" are address hyperbumps that emerge from the behaviours of the population it serves. In that respect it's no different to any other Problem Transforming Organisation (PTO) in the ecosystem. The difference is that it's scope is "State" problems.  It will follow the same PTO Process (Problems -> Transformations -> Outcomes) but will be scoped to the State level.
