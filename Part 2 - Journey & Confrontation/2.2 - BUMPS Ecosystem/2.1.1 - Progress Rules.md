# Progress Rules
- The rules of progress are defined by the environment (BUMPSPACE). The global BUMPSPACE is defined by our biosphere, such as the laws of physics, chemistry, biology, and the laws of thermodynamics. 

- The local BUMPSPACE is defined by the artificial environment, such as the laws of economics, politics, and social dynamics.

- Private BUMPSpaces define their own rules, such as the laws of finance, marketing, and technology. Organizations can define their own internal rules based on their mission, vision, and values.

- Private BUMPSpaces naturally inherit the rules of the global BUMPSPACE.
