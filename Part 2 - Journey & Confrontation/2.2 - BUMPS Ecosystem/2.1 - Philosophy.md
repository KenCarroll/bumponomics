# BUMPONOMICS Philosophy

# All Progress is Problem Transforming

Karl Popper wrote that "All progress is problem transforming". Bumponomics extends this idea to include all progress is problem transforming. Creating new problems is a necessary part of progress. Critically progress is a vector of change. It can be positive or negative. Progress is not a constant. It is a dynamic process. 

# Progress Vector

# Solving vs Evolving
The are very different. Solving creates a temporary state of being. Think of the world we have created by solving problems. This is the world of products and services. 

Evolving means you evolve into a new state of being. Think of the world we have created by evolving problems. This is the biologic world of organisms and societies. Humans evolve into new species over time to adapt to their changing environment. 

Products and Services don't evolve. They are redesigned and reimagined and redeployed. They have no capacity to evolve.
