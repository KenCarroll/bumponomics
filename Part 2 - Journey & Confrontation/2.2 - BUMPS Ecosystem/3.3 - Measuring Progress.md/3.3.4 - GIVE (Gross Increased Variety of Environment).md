# GIVE (Gross Increased Variety of Environment)

The Measure of Evolution.

This is the ultimate test of the Bumpivore.

Question: After the problem was transformed, does the system have more options, more diverse parts, and more capability for exaptation than before?

Contrast: A monopoly reduces GIVE (less variety). A healthy ecosystem increases GIVE.
