# State of Being (PDST)

## Participants
Number of participants

## D = Demise (Didn't survive)
How many participants didn't survive or are in the process of dying.

## S = Survival (Survive)
How many participants survived or are in the process of surviving.

## T = Thriving (Thrived)
How many participants thrived or are in the process of thriving.

## Visualisation of PDST

Layers :

- Seeing how many people in each state of being.

- Seeing how many problems in each state of being over time.
